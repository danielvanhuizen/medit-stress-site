About Us

Medi Stress is a mobile IV wellness service focused on safe, convenient vitamin and hydration infusions. We tailor each session to your needs after a brief health screening.

All treatments are prepared and administered by a registered nurse using sterile equipment and medical-grade products. We operate by appointment only.
