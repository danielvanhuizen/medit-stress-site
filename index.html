About Us

Medi-T Stress is a mobile IV wellness clinic providing personalised vitamin and hydration infusions in the comfort of your own space. We specialise in safe, evidence-based intravenous (IV) therapy to help you recover faster, boost your energy, support your immune system and feel your best — whether you are an athlete, busy professional, parent on the go or recovering from a big night out.

All treatments are prepared and administered by a registered nurse, using sterile equipment and medical-grade products. We come to you in the greater Durban and Umhlanga area, by appointment only.
